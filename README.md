# Contextly

> **Bring context to your team—when and where you need it—without the tool-switching.**

Modern cross-functional teams (Product Managers, Developers, Designers, and Engineering Leads) waste hours jumping between Slack, Jira, Notion, and Google Drive just to stay aligned. Finding a status update or tracking down the right person with the right context usually means digging through endless threads or interrupting teammates.

**Contextly fixes this.** It is an AI-powered assistant that integrates directly into Slack to passively synthesize updates across your entire tool stack, surfacing actionable insights seamlessly.

---

## 🧩 The Problem
* **Information Silos:** Updates are scattered across Jira tickets, Notion docs, and Slack threads.
* **High Friction:** Team members constantly interrupt each other or swap tabs to find basic status updates.
* **Alignment Lag:** Decision-making slows down because context is lost in the noise.

## ✨ The Solution
Contextly intelligently aggregates data from your workspace tools and serves as a centralized context layer inside Slack. 

* **For PMs:** Effortlessly track progress without micromanaging.
* **For Devs:** Quickly catch up on technical decisions and blockers.
* **For Team Leads:** Get a bird's-eye view of team health and project status instantly.
